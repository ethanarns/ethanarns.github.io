About me

I am a 3D Visual Effects artist, graduated with a BA in Animation from Loyola Marymount University's School of Film and Television. The intersection of art and technology has always been my passion and creative outlet. I have created the entirety of visual effects of over 8 films, as well as designed and 3D modeled for solo and group videogame projects. My professional experience includes rig removal, rotoscoping, compositing, and motion graphics. In addition, I also have work in virtual reality games and videos.

In addition, I also have professional experience as a full stack Web Developer. I minored in Computer Science, and have a solid knowledge of Java, Javascript, C++, C-Sharp, Unreal 4, and Python. I taught Python and Java coding to students for 3 years as an assistant instructor. My diverse set of skills in programming and artistic design coupled with my great passion and work ethic makes me a driven artist eager to make his mark on the world.
